Lunch Recipe For Turkey Sandwich

Lunch, for many people, is an afternoon or midday break where they can enjoy and have a meal during this time. A majority of Americans usually eat lunch when they are on lunch break during work, and there are also many that eat lunch at home! Lunch provides a brief break from the day so that you can allow yourself to enjoy a delicious and fulfilling meal. This allows you to re-energize yourself for the rest of the day and not become hungry until dinner time. Lunch can range from a wide variety of foods from lunch meat sandwiches, to burgers, to numerous different delicious salads, and much more.

In this Lunch Dish Recipe webpage, you will be learning about how to make a delicious and tasty turkey sandwich. A turkey sandwich can range in a very wide range of selections that can be added onto the sandwich. Some may prefer to add different condiments, different vegetables, other kinds of meats, use different breads, and much more! In this recipe, this turkey sandwich will be nothing but tasty and very easy to make. It will provide a nice flavor and taste to your mouth when you make it and eat it. Let's get ready to start learning on how to craft and perfect the best turkey sandwich possible for lunch!

Turkey Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients Needed:

French or Italian Loaf Bread
Mayonnaise
Mustard (Optional)
American Cheese (Slices)
Oven Roasted Turkey (Slices)
Lettuce
Tomato
Red Onion
Banana Peppers
Red Wine Vinegar (Optional)
Olive Oil (Optional)
Salt
Black Pepper
Italian Seasoning
Garlic Powder

Recipe Steps:

1. First, make your vegetables ready to slice and chop.
2. Second, chop the tomatoes and onions into slice pieces, and chop the lettuce into small pieces like a salad.
3. Third, slice your french or italian bread in half, but not all the way through. Make sure your bread is able to open up in half.
4. Fourth, spread mayonnaise on both sides of the bread.
5. Fifth, sprinkle all of the seasonings from the ingredients list onto the mayonnaise. Sprinkle according to your taste.
6. Sixth, put turkey slices (slices depend on size of bread) onto the bread. Then, put American cheese on top of the turkey slices.
7. Seventh, add your tomato slices onto the sandwich. Then, add slices of onions. Then add your banana peppers. Add chopped lettuce to the other side of the bread.
8. Eighth, add a mixture of olive oil and red wine vinegar as your flavorful condiment, or you can add mustard.
9. Finally, on this last step, fold your sandwich, and cut it according to what you'll eat. Enjoy the delicious lunch turkey sandwich!
